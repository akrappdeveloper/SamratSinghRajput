ABOUT ME

Personal Background

I am Samrat Singh Rajput, born on 15th October 2003 in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India. At 21 years old, I’ve established myself as a social media influencer and YouTuber, passionate about creating love story videos that resonate with my audience. I’m also an entrepreneur with big dreams of expanding my business ventures.

Family Background

I hail from a close-knit family in Lucknow. My father, Thakur Badshah Kushwaha, and my mother, Sunila Devi, have always supported my creative journey. My brother, Aman Rajput, is my constant companion and motivator. My family’s encouragement has been the backbone of my success.

Educational Background

I completed my schooling at Sri Ayodhya Singh Memorial Inter College, Lucknow, in 2024. Currently, I’m pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University, Lucknow, balancing my education with my career as a content creator.

Interests & Hobbies

I’m passionate about singing and traveling, which keep me inspired. My favorite foods are Sahi Paneer, Puri, Kheer, and Jalebi – a perfect blend of flavors! I’m unmarried and fully dedicated to my career and personal growth.

More About Me

I’m a storyteller at heart, using my platform to connect with people through emotional and relatable content. My journey as a content creator has taught me resilience and creativity, and I’m always looking for new ways to inspire my audience.

MY JOURNEY

Starting as a Content Creator

My journey began with a passion for storytelling. I started Samrat Rajput Creation to produce love story videos that capture the essence of emotions. Over time, my content gained traction, and I built a loyal audience on social media platforms.

Entrepreneurial Venture

Alongside my content creation, I founded Samrat Royal Collection in Lucknow, offering customized printing services like T-shirt and mug printing. My vision is to expand this business into a larger empire.

Future Goals

I aim to continue creating impactful content that inspires others while growing my business.

My dream is to make Samrat Royal Collection a household name.

I also plan to collaborate with brands and creators worldwide.

Expand my digital presence by launching a mobile app for Samrat Rajput Creation to reach a wider audience.
